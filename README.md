## 📌 Executive Summary

This project executes a comprehensive **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** and **Statistical Hypothesis Testing** suite on retail banking transactional data. Moving beyond standard reporting, this analysis proactively identifies systemic vulnerabilities, isolates extreme capital flight, and mathematically validates internal banking assumptions regarding customer liquidity retention.

By engineering mathematical anomaly thresholds and conducting rigorous cohort testing, this framework provides actionable, data-backed strategies for automated **Anti-Money Laundering (AML)** monitoring and targeted marketing efficiency.

---

## 🎯 Core Business Objectives

* **🔍 Isolate Financial Anomalies:** Engineer statistical boundaries (IQR) to separate standard operational noise from high-risk capital flight.
* **⚖️ Validate Liquidity Drivers:** Mathematically test internal assumptions regarding transaction frequency and capital retention.
* **🗺️ Map Portfolio Exposure:** Expose structural vulnerabilities by mapping Assets Under Management (AUM) against standard FICO credit tiers.
* **🛡️ Synthesize Suspicion Scoring:** Develop multi-vector risk profiles to drastically reduce false positives in AML investigations.

---

## 🚀 Key Strategic Insights

### 1️⃣ High-Volume Transactors Do Not Drive Liquidity
An independent right-tailed T-test (`p=0.86`) mathematically disproved the assumption that high-frequency transactional behavior correlates with higher account balances. 
> **💡 Business Impact:** High-volume accounts act as operational clearinghouses with lower retained capital. Premium wealth-management marketing must be strictly routed to lower-volume, high-balance "wealth-parking" accounts to eliminate marketing waste.

### 2️⃣ Capital Distribution is Structurally Stable
A One-Way ANOVA test (`p=0.20`) across primary account segments (Savings, Current, Credit, Loan) revealed that the bank's liquidity is uniformly distributed. 
> **💡 Business Impact:** The portfolio is adequately insulated against sector-specific shocks. No single account type holds a statistically disproportionate share of the total capital.

### 3️⃣ Clear Delineation of Risk Exposure
Applying a dual-axis Risk Matrix and an AUM-by-FICO distribution mapping successfully isolated the bank's safest cash-engine products from critical liabilities. 
> **💡 Business Impact:** This framework allows for dynamic credit tiering, enabling the business to proactively tighten lending parameters if capital begins shifting heavily into subprime FICO bands.
